Interview Guidelines

Please review these important requirements before starting

Camera & Proctoring

  • Keep your camera on throughout the entire interview
  • Ensure good lighting and clear visibility of your face
  • Only one person should be visible in frame
  • Your face must be clearly visible at all times

Browser & Tab Rules

  • Stay on this interview tab at all times
  • No switching to other tabs or windows
  • Don't minimize or hide the browser window
  • All focus must remain on the interview interface

Violation Policy

  • Multiple Faces: Maximum 2 violations allowed
  • No Face Detected: Maximum 5 violations allowed
  • Tab Switching: Maximum 2 violations allowed
  • 2nd violation of any type will result in automatic termination

Recording Best Practices

  • Speak clearly and maintain a professional tone
  • Answer each question completely and thoughtfully
  • Take your time – there's no rush
  • Total interview duration: approximately 20 minutes

Technical Requirements

  • Stable internet connection (minimum 2 Mbps recommended)
  • Modern browser (Chrome)
  • Working microphone and camera
